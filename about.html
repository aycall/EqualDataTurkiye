About

Academic project

This page has been created for a final-year project at Ankara Science University.

This platform aims to highlight developments at both global and national levels by addressing gender equality through a data-driven approach. Indicators relating to key areas such as education, health and political participation are compiled from reliable data sources and presented in a simple, clear and comparable format.

The objective is to transform complex datasets into accessible visualisations, enabling users to analyse cross-country differences and raise awareness of gender inequality. In this regard, the platform aims to contribute to more informed assessments through data-driven insights.

To create a future where gender equality can be monitored through data-driven, transparent analyses, and where balanced representation is ensured in education, healthcare and decision-making processes across all societies.

To make gender inequality measurable by utilising reliable national and international data sources; to raise awareness and contribute to evidence-based decision-making processes by presenting this data through clear, comparable and interactive visualisations.
