Knowledge Academy

Compact guides on how our portal aligns with primary sources — so you can read maps and tables with confidence and cite them correctly.

WEF pillars on this portal

Education (Educational Attainment), Health and Survival, and Political Empowerment correspond to Global Gender Gap Report subindices (0–1 scale). Narrative excerpts on-site follow the Forum’s definitions; numerical cells on comparator maps must be checked against each year’s official country spreadsheets.

Global Gender Gap Report 2025 — WEF Publications

Türkiye national context

TÜİK’s Women in Statistics dossier complements global indices with Türkiye-specific tables (education attainment, labour, health summaries). Figures shown in infographic-style charts are illustrative; update from the latest bulletin when auditing for academic work.

TÜİK · Women in Statistics 2025 (PDF)

International reference series

Maternal mortality timelines use World Bank open indicator metadata—always reconcile model revisions and vintages noted in the download. OECD gender aggregates can support labour-pay narratives where annotated on-site.

World Bank · SH.STA.MMRT · OECD Gender Data

Methodology notes & how to cite

  • Use the WEF publication year and table / data extract you downloaded when citing comparator scores.
  • For TÜİK blocks, cite the publication title, year, and table number from official PDFs.
  • World Bank downloads list source label and license — retain them in appendix or footnote.
  • This portal explains concepts; it is not itself a canonical data warehouse — primary tables remain the authority.

References & data sources

This page mirrors the short source lines under each chart on the Gender Data Portal home page. Use the matching numbered bibliography entries (APA 7, English) when you write up results; every title below links to the primary publisher.

Sources by portal visualization

“Home page” means the landing experience with explore cards, choropleths, and expandable detail panels fed by the same-origin templates.

  • Global Gender Gap choropleths (Educational Attainment, Health & Survival, Political Empowerment). Country cells and subindex definitions follow the World Economic Forum (2025) report and data extract. Bibliography: [1]
  • Education infographic (levels, enrolment, STEM, poverty, academic posts). National tables and percentages — TÜİK, Women in Statistics 2025. Bibliography: [2]
  • Women’s labour force & employment trend chart (Plotly). TÜİK Data Portal labour releases; reinforced by Women in Statistics [2]; OECD Gender Data Portal and OECD Türkiye pages for cross-country context. Bibliography: [2–5]
  • Gender pay-gap comparison band (OECD / EU context). Illustrative bands only—verify against OECD gender-pay releases and Turkish official labour statistics (Women in Statistics and TÜİK Data Portal releases; see bibliography [2–4]) before citing exact percentages. Bibliography: [2–4]
  • Maternal mortality narrative & Türkiye timeline. Modelled series from World Bank SH.STA.MMRT for the plotted profile; reconcile with Türkiye’s official vital-statistics releases when auditing for research. Bibliography: [6]
  • Health dashboard cards (life expectancy, maternal mortality highlight, care access). National snapshot values — Women in Statistics 2025. Bibliography: [2]
  • Political representation hierarchy (ambassadors → council of ministers). WEF Political Empowerment framing [1] plus Türkiye institutional shares from Women in Statistics [2]. Bibliography: [1–2]
